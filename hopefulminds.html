About the Initiative

The HopeFul Mind Initiative is Ascendia Management's flagship Corporate Social Responsibility program focused on mental health awareness and community well-being in Myanmar. We believe that mental wellness is fundamental to personal and professional success.

In a society where mental health is often stigmatized, our initiative aims to break down barriers, provide education, and offer support systems for individuals and communities across Yangon.
